Person A: Hello, Doctor. I've been having a sore throat for the past two weeks.
Person B: I see. Have you had any other symptoms like fever or cough?
Person A: Yes, I've had a mild fever and a bit of a cough.
Person B: Alright, let's have a look at your throat. Please open your mouth wide and say "ah."
Person A: Ah.
Person B: Your throat looks quite inflamed. I think we should do a throat swab to check for any infections.
Person A: Okay, that sounds like a good idea. What should I do in the meantime?
Person B: Try to rest, drink plenty of fluids, and avoid any irritants like smoke. I'll prescribe you some medication to help with the pain and inflammation.
